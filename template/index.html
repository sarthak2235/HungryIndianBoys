About Us

We are a group of two Indian-Australian boys who love food and exploring Melbourne's best restaurants. We're passionate about sharing our experiences with others, and we believe that everyone should have the opportunity to try delicious food from all over the world. We're always on the lookout for new restaurants to try, and we're always happy to give our honest opinions. We're not afraid to be critical, but we're also not afraid to sing the praises of a great restaurant. We believe that food is more than just sustenance. It's a way to connect with people from different cultures, and it's a way to experience new things. We hope that our reviews will help you find the best restaurants in Melbourne, and we hope that you'll join us on our journey of culinary exploration.
